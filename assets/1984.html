1984 by George Orwell

A chilling dystopian novel that explores the consequences of totalitarianism, mass surveillance, and repressive government control. Set in a bleak future where Big Brother watches every move, “1984” follows Winston Smith, a man who dares to question the regime and seeks truth in a world built on lies. Orwell’s masterpiece is a powerful warning about the loss of freedom and the dangers of unchecked political authority. A must-read classic that feels more relevant than ever.
